  • Is it safe?
    • Yes, it is completely safe: we don't use bots or any other program. We require the information that is needed just to log in. Never tell anyone your secret question!

    • It takes approximately 3-5 hours to finish a boost.
  • How and when can I pay?
    • We don't want you to pay and wait for your boost. So you pay only when the booster contacts you. You can pay in any of the following ways: PayPal, Yandex.Money, QIWI

    • No, the booster will not communicate with your friends. Also the booster will not add any person to your friend list. If it is a major issue, we can swap regions so your friends list is clear and they can't see you online.
  • Can I play other games on my Battle.net?
    • You shouldn't log in as it can disconnect the booster. However, if you see that the booster is offline, you can go ahead and play even Overwatch (except ladder).
