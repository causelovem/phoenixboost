  • Is it safe?
    • Yes, it is completely safe: we don't use bots or any other program. We require the information that is needed just to log in. Never tell anyone your secret question!
  • How much time will my boost require to be complete?
    • It depends on a lot of things: the state of current meta, luck involved and whether it is a beginning or end of the season. But it takes approximately week and a half to finish a boost.
  • Should I have any deck on my account?
    • Yes, you need to have at least one deck from our website for Legend boost.
  • How and when can I pay?
    • We don't want you to pay and wait for your boost. So you pay only when the booster contacts you. You can pay in any of the following ways: PayPal, Yandex.Money, QIWI
  • Will the booster communicate with my friends?
    • No, the booster will not communicate with your friends. Also the booster will not add any person to your friend list.
  • Can I play other games on my Battle.net?
    • You shouldn't log in as it can disconnect the booster. However, if you see that the booster is offline, you can go ahead and play even Hearthstone (except ladder).
  • On what servers is the service available?
    • Service is available on all servers.
  • How do I contact you if my questions haven't been answered?
    • Fell free to contact us in any of the following ways: email support@phoenixboost.com or skype phoenixboost support.
