FAQ
Is it safe?
Yes, it is completely safe: we don't use bots, cheats or any other program. We require the information that is needed just to log in. Never tell anyone your secret question!
How much time will my boost require to be complete?
It takes 1-3 days to complete any boost, but most we do in less than 12 hours.
On what platform is the service available?
Service is available only on PC.
How and when can I pay?
We don't want you to pay and wait for your boost. So you pay only when the booster contacts you. You can pay in any of the following ways: PayPal, Yandex.Money, QIWI
Will the booster communicate with my friends?
No, the booster will not communicate with your friends. If it is a major issue, we can turn on offline mood or swap regions so your friends list is clear and they can't see you online.
Can I play other games on my Battle.net?
You shouldn't log in as it can disconnect the booster. However, if you see that the booster is offline, you can go ahead and play even Overwatch (except ladder).
On what servers is the service available?
Service is available on all servers.
How do I contact you if my questions haven't been answered?
Fell free to contact us in any of the following ways: email support@phoenixboost.com or skype phoenixboost support (live:support_49862).
